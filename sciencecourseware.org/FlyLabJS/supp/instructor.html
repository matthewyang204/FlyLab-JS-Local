Getting Access to the Information for Instructors

To send an email request for access to the information for instructors, click this link:

Email Request for Instructor's Information

Include your name, the institution where you teach, and the email address where you want to receive the login credentials. This information will be sent by a human, so please allow 24 hours for a response. There is no charge for this information.

Once you receive the login credentials by email, return to this page, click on the button in the header above, and enter the user name and password.

You may share the information with other instructors, but please do not post the login credentials in any public site on the internet.

Thank you!
